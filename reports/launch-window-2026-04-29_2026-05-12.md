3. The market is treating HITZ as a hub asset, which is exactly the position you want for a token with bounded concentration: it earns swap fees passively without anyone needing to hold a position.

## Things worth watching

1. **GDM22NKY and GDFCIFZL together = 26 txs (22% of total).** If those are bots, fine — they're providing arb pressure that tightens pricing. If one of them goes dormant, daily activity could drop noticeably.
2. **0 endpoint trades is striking.** No-one is finishing a swap holding HITZ. Either nobody wants to *hold* HITZ yet, or the UI's SmartSwap is routing exits out automatically. Worth confirming which in product analytics.
3. **Aqua's `execute_arb` direction was XLM → HITZ → USDC → XLM** (a profitable cycle on Apr 29 / May 6). If those cycles persist, spreads between the HITZ pools are wider than market-equilibrium — pool LP yields are real, but it also means there's room to tighten.
